Organization Goals

Phase 1

Live recordings of local musicians and artists from the Cincinnati area.

Phase 2

Recorded artists are supported in the community with gigs at Historic Findlay Market and other select venues around the city.

Phase 3

A commemorative compilation vinyl pressing of the artists.

Phase 4

Proceeds from the album, live shows, and donations fund the purchase of instruments, subsidize lessons and mentorships for the underprivileged youth in the Cincinnati Tri State area.
